About Prajus Designs

Premium boutique tailoring & aari embroidery studio

Our Story

Prajus Designs blends traditional craftsmanship with contemporary tailoring. Our artisans specialize in custom blouses, couture gowns, kidswear, and sustainable saree upcycling, all finished with signature aari embroidery.

Each project begins with a personalised consultation to understand your event, silhouettes, and embellishment preferences. From sketch to final fit, we obsess over details so you receive heirloom-worthy garments that feel as good as they look.

Beyond studio orders, we teach structured aari courses that preserve the craft while empowering a new generation of designers.

Handcrafted

Bespoke patterns, hand finishing, and curated embellishments for every client.

Quality

Premium fabrics, precise construction, and multi-stage fitting checks.

Sustainable

Thoughtful saree upcycling, reuse of heirloom textiles, and minimal waste.

What We Craft

Custom Blouses

Princess-cut, patchwork, corset backs, and 5D aari panels tailored to your drape.

Maxi & Gowns

Circular, straight, and lehenga-style silhouettes with hidden corsetry and luxe finishes.

Kidswear

Play-proof frocks, lehengas, and sibling sets with breathable linings.

Saree Upcycling

Transform heirloom sarees into kurtis, maxis, lehengas, or décor accessories.

Aari Embroidery

From minimalist borders to bridal couture with zardosi, kundan, and 5D petals.

Aari Courses

Structured modules covering foundations, bridal concepts, and patchwork mastery.
