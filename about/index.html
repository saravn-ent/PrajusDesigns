About Prajus Designs

Premium Boutique Tailoring & Aari Embroidery Studio

Our Story

Welcome to Prajus Designs, where tradition meets contemporary style. We are a premium boutique tailoring and aari embroidery studio dedicated to crafting exquisite custom designs that celebrate individuality and artistry.

With years of experience in tailoring and intricate aari embroidery, our skilled artisans transform your vision into reality. Whether you're seeking a custom blouse, elegant maxi gown, or sustainable saree upcycling, we bring passion and precision to every piece.

Our mission is to preserve and promote the art of traditional aari embroidery while creating contemporary designs that resonate with modern fashion enthusiasts.

Handcrafted

Every piece is meticulously crafted with attention to every detail by skilled artisans.

Quality

We use premium fabrics and materials to ensure lasting beauty and comfort.

Sustainable

Our saree upcycling service promotes eco-friendly fashion practices.

Services We Offer

Custom Blouse Designs

Pattern, patchwork, princess-cut, and crop-top styles tailored to your specifications.

Maxi Gowns

Elegant circular, straight, and lehenga-style maxis for all occasions.

Kidswear

Comfortable and colorful frocks, lehengas, and festive sets for children.

Saree Upcycling

Transform old sarees into beautiful kurtis, lehengas, and maxis.

Aari Embroidery

Intricate aari work from basic to advanced, including bridal designs.

Aari Courses

Professional training in aari embroidery, tailoring, and design techniques.
